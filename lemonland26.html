Hello Lemons,

It's with pleasure that we can finally announce the return of our zestival for 2026!

As with all previous years it will be happening at the rear-end of the summer, and we'll be returning to the delightful flat fields of Norfolk to celebrate.

Featuring all things Lemon Lounge - the warm embrace of the LL sound system, lemon affiliated DJs & live music, and of course the plethora of zesty activities you have come to expect (and some you haven't) + much improved catering!

🧖‍♀️

Our partnership with Wondering Wild Spa was a big hit last year - so we're continuing the relationship and will have an 8 person sauna fired up and running all weekend. It's the real deal - hand built in Lithuania and maintained by a team of sauna lover lemons. You'll be able to get access by buying a weekend wristband when you arrive at the festival.

⛺

On top of that we also have nine 6 person glamping tents available to rent. Each tent has 1 double bed and 4 singles with all bedding and mains power. They are priced per whole tent so you can decide the groups you want to stay with. We're expecting these to fly out too so go get em.

We're making the tickets as cheap as we possibly can in order to break even for the weekend. That being said if the price is a barrier to access and you would really like to come then drop us a message and we will do our best to help.

We're launching tickets today and sending this flyer out to everyone who has bought tickets to Lemon Land in the past, as well as our closer community. We're only selling 250 tickets and we expect it to sell out so grab them up while you can. As before, with the festival being a small/intimate affair, please send us a message if you'd like to come with a big group.

The festival will be the same rough format as previous years:

Friday
Gates open at 4pm. We'll arrange a big lemon pot luck feast and ask you to bring something to contribute if you can. We will have some basic reheating facilities and the theme this year is GREEK

Saturday
The sound system will be switched on at midday, and we'll kick off the various workshops and activities. We'll have our usual slew of DJs, and a few late night surprises if all goes to plan

Sunday
Live music all day and we're in the midst of curating our best lime-up yet. Please do stay for the ceremony in the evening and a final dance, before heading off on Monday morning

🚗

Travel-wise, if you're driving we'll send an exact pin drop out nearer the time, it's in a small village near Thetford which is a 2hr 30min drive from SE London. Please do car share if you can!

If you'll be getting the train, head to Thetford which is the nearest well connected station. It will be a cheap-ish taxi from there to the site. There is a 2 hour train from Kings Cross if you're coming from London.

That's about it! Any questions get in touch
