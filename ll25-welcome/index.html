The below timings are from doors open until the Lounge music stops.

Friday | 17:00 - 01:00

Saturday | 12:00 - 02:00

Sunday | 12:00 - 00:00

Accreditation will close at 21:00 on Friday night and 17:00 on Saturday, so please plan your arrival around these times.

Full weekend music and activity programme to follow. If you missed the info when buying your ticket, the general arc of the weekend is:

Friday - Pot Luck Feast & DJs in Lounge

Saturday - DJs in Lounge and activities all day until late

Sunday - Live bands, activities and 🍋 ceremony…

Sunday night is always a special one, so we hope you’ll stay and then we’ll be out of there on Monday morning.


🫠 SAUNA 🫠

Our 8 person sauna will be fired up and running all weekend, all thanks to the amazing Wondering Wild Spa.

Unlimited weekend sauna wristbands will be available for £20 and can be purchased on arrival at accreditation. The sauna will be open over the weekend and will operate on a ‘just show up’ basis (no need to book a slot in advance). Swimsuits are required for the sauna but there will be nude sessions from 11am-12pm each day for those that wish to partake.

If you plan to use the sauna, please remember to bring a towel to sit on so we can keep it clean. It will be open at the following times:

Saturday | 10:00 - 18:00

Sunday | 10:00 - 18:00


⛺️ YURTS ⛺️

For those who’ve booked a yurt: it will be made up with bedding for six people (one double bed and four singles) and a carpeted floor. Each tent also comes with its own power supply for phone charging, etc.

🍽️ FRIDAY NIGHT POT LUCK DINNER 🍽️

As per tradition, we’re arranging a big lemon pot luck feast and this year’s theme is Italian!

We ask you to bring something to contribute if you can. Please make sure your dish is clearly labelled - labels should include the dish name and note any allergens.

We will have some basic reheating facilities available and any tupperware will be cleaned post-feast ready for collection.
